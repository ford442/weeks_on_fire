# Ideas

This folder is for raw brainstorming and high-level creative thinking.

## How to use
- Create new `.md` files for big ideas (e.g. `episode-03-concept.md`)
- Use `backlog.md` for quick notes and "maybe later" items
- Once an idea is ready, move the relevant parts into `episodes/`, `characters/`, or `songs/`

## Suggested files
- `episode-concepts.md` – overall series arcs and episode pitches
- `song-ideas.md` – track shortlist + emotional fit
- `visual-themes.md` – recurring motifs, color palettes, style references
- `backlog.md` – quick capture of fleeting ideas
