# ZERO GRAVITY & BAD VINTAGES 
**A "Weeks of Fire" Short**

**INT. ORBITAL GREENHOUSE / SPACESHIP - NIGHT**

A sleek, sterile white spaceship interior overgrown with lush, aggressive Earth ivy. Earth looms massive and blue out the reinforced viewport. 

RUBELLA and SCARLET (identical, both wearing immaculate black lace dresses despite being in orbit) lounge on zero-gravity chaise lounges. They are both sipping violently red wine from crystal goblets that inexplicably don't float away. 

Between them floats a spherical, white ROBOT HEAD with a friendly, glowing LED smiley face.

Suddenly, a bright NEON BLUE LASER beam shoots past the window, followed by a NEON RED one. 

SCARLET
(Sighs, swirls her wine)
They're back. 

RUBELLA
I told you we shouldn't have parked so close to the Halloween Nebula. It’s entirely out of season.

Outside the glass, two massive, glowing LASER SNAKES coil around the ship's communication array, hissing sparks into the vacuum of space. 

SCARLET
It’s not the season, Rubella. It’s the pheromones in your perfume. It attracts reptiles with high-energy directed weapons. 

RUBELLA
It’s a classic musk. And besides, I think they’re just jealous of the Cabernet. 

The ROBOT HEAD chimes in. Its voice is overly cheerful, like a customer service representative trapped in a blender.

ROBOT HEAD
*Alert! Hull integrity is currently at eighty-two percent. Would you like me to deploy the anti-snake countermeasures, or play some light jazz?*

RUBELLA
Jazz, please. The smooth kind. The lasers are giving me a migraine. 

ROBOT HEAD
*Deploying Kenny G. Please remain calm.*

Smooth saxophone music filters through the cabin. Outside, a yellow laser beam grazes the glass, leaving a scorch mark. 

Scarlet casually leans over and taps the glass with perfectly manicured nails. 

SCARLET
Shoo. Go be mythological somewhere else. 

A TABBY CAT floats slowly past them, entirely unfazed by the lack of gravity. It bats lazily at a floating drop of spilled red wine.

RUBELLA
Look at Bartholomew. He doesn't care about the structural integrity of the ship. Be more like Bartholomew.

SCARLET
Bartholomew doesn't have to pay the insurance premium on a Class-4 Orbital Cruiser, Rubella. 

RUBELLA
(Takes a slow sip)
True. 

A massive jolt rocks the ship. The wine in their glasses ripples, but defies physics to stay in the bowl. 

ROBOT HEAD
*Correction. Hull integrity is now at forty-one percent. Have a nice day!*

SCARLET
(Looks at her glass)
You know, the zero gravity really ruins the legs on this vintage. It tastes entirely too... floaty.

RUBELLA
I was just thinking that. Next time, we take the Lamborghini.

SCARLET
To space?

RUBELLA
Why not? It has leather seats. 

Outside, the laser snakes intertwine, charging up a massive, blinding rainbow blast. Rubella and Scarlet don't even blink. They just clink their glasses together.

RUBELLA / SCARLET
(In unison)
Cheers.

**SMASH CUT TO BLACK.**

**TITLE CARD: WEEKS OF FIRE**
