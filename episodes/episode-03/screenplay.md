### Scene 3 — "Laser Battle Erupts" *(0:20–0:35)*

```
EXT. BACKYARD — CONTINUOUS

The serpents EXPLODE into motion.

SERPENT ONE fires a beam of CYAN LIGHT from its open jaw —
it punches through a plastic pumpkin, which spins off the 
fence post and lands in the grass, still glowing.

SERPENT TWO coils back and fires MAGENTA — 
it scorches the garden shed wall, leaving a perfect 
circular burn mark. The smell: ozone and singed wood.

The cat dives behind a decorative hay bale.

Then, something unexpected:

The PLASTIC SKELETON on the fence post — the cheap one —
begins to move.

Slowly at first. Then with purpose.

It UNZIPS itself from the fence post with mechanical 
determination. Its jaw drops open in a permanent grin 
that has suddenly become genuinely menacing.

It reaches down and picks up a stray CYAN LASER BEAM 
that has bounced off the shed wall and is rolling 
along the ground (somehow). 

It fires back.

THE SKELETON dances as it fires — a ridiculous, irresistible 
skeleton two-step that perfectly matches the chaos. 
The choreography is undeniable. 

The cat watches from behind the hay bale, horrified.
```
