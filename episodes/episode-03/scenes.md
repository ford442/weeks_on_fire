# Episode 3 – Scenes

## Scene: "Laser Serpent Skirmish" (Halloween Pull-Back Reveal)

**Duration**: ~1:10 seconds  
**Location**: Quiet suburban backyard / empty lot at night  
**Time of Year**: Halloween night  
**Tone**: Over-the-top chaos meets deadpan suburban normalcy  
**New Cinematic Joke Element**: Porch lights suddenly turn on revealing a completely unfazed pizza delivery guy standing there with a pizza box  
**Music**: Soft piano (X-Files style) enters at ~0:40, swells during pull-back

**Description**  
A sleek black cat with glowing yellow eyes pads into the backyard. It sniffs around Halloween decorations, tail high, completely unaware of what’s about to happen.

**Camera**  
Low tracking shot following the cat.

### 0:10 – 0:20  “Discovery at the Side of the Yard”

**Description**  
The cat reaches the bushes near the shed and freezes. Two glowing serpents are coiled there. One slowly raises its head. The cat’s fur puffs up.

**Camera**  
Slow push-in on cat, then rack focus to the snakes.

**Description**  
The snakes explode into action, firing cyan and magenta lasers. The plastic skeleton suddenly animates, grabs a fallen laser, and starts blasting back while doing a ridiculous skeleton dance. The cat watches in shock.

**Camera**  
Wide chaotic shot with quick cuts to skeleton dancing and firing.

**Grok Imagine Prompt**

### 0:35 – 0:45  “Pizza Guy Appears (Deadpan Reveal)"

**Description**  
Suddenly the house porch lights flick on with a loud *click*. A bored pizza delivery guy in a red uniform stands on the porch holding a pizza box, completely unfazed by the laser battle happening 20 feet away. He checks his phone, then looks up like this is just another normal delivery.

**Camera**  
Quick cut to the porch, then slow push-in on the pizza guy’s deadpan face as lasers fly behind him.

**Sound Design**  
- Loud porch light click  
- Soft "ding" from his phone  
- Distant laser zaps continue
